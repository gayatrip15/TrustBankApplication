Trust Bank Help Center

1. How do I log in to my account?

Go to the TrustBank homepage and click on the “Login” button. Enter your account number and password to access your dashboard.

2. How can I transfer money?

After logging in, go to the “Transfer Money” section. Enter the recipient’s account number, amount, and description, then click “Submit”.

3. What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the “Forgot Password” link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password using your registered email or mobile number.
